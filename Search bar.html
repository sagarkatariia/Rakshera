Geography

Rakshera has an average elevation of 227 metres (745 ft). It is situated on Bank
of Yamuna River (Jamna), 16 km (11.7 miles) East of Samalkha, 82 kilometres (45 mi)
from the capital Delhi, 154 km (84 miles) south of Etawah and 196 km (110 miles)
southwest of Chandigarh


Demographics

According to the 2011 India census, Rakshera had a population of 5,710. Males are
2,921 and females are 2789 . Rakshera has an average literacy rate of 78%, higher
than the national average of 61.5%: male literacy is 78%, and female literacy is
69%. In Rakshera, 20% of the population is under 6 years of age.


Market

Rakshera takes the position of ward no. 5 in Samalkha. It is in possession of
large grain, jaggery and wood markets, one of the largest raw food supplier in
the sub-district Samalkha, Panipat. It also receives a high influx of customers
from neighboring districts likeBaghpat or Uttar Pardesh.
